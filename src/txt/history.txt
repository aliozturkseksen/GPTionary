What is the word used to describe a person who is always in a bad mood?

Grump: a person who is habitually irritable or ill-tempered. 
Trivia: The word "grump" has been used since the early 1800s. 
Example Sentences: 
1. My brother is such a grump, he never wants to do anything fun. 
2. The grump refused to help his neighbor with their yard work. 
3. I'm not in the mood to deal with that grump today.
